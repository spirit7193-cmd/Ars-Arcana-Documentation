There are times when Number Clusters must be combined and changed, almost as though it has been transmuted. In this section, we will look over the various incants that perform this necessary function.

**SINVERA**

**SYNTAX**

_Akashic -> Number/Vector, Number/Vector_

_Sinvera always requires EITHER 2 Numbers OR 2 Vectors. Addition cannot happen cross-Clusters._

_Akashic <- Number/Vector_

_Sinvera is the incant that signifies "addition, total, an increasing of a value by another". If Numbers are provided, an addition is performed and the resulting Number is returned. If Vectors are provided, an addition is performed and the resulting Vector is returned. Whatever is returned is added to the top of the Akashic Stack._

**Example Spells:**

ETE BIEGEA ETE ESILIB SINVERA DEBUG -> Allows the caster to see the Akashic and view the combined result of their head position and their view direction.

This result represents the position directly in front of the caster's head. By changing DEBUG to FIRUGEA DEBUG, the caster could see what block is directly in front of them.

**SINVARA**

**SYNTAX**

_Akashic -> Number/Vector, Number/Vector_

_Sinvara always requires EITHER 2 Numbers OR 2 Vectors. Subtraction cannot happen cross-Clusters. The value that is being subtracted should always be stated before the value that should be subtracted._

_Akashic <- Number/Vector_

_Sinvara is the incant that signifies "subtraction, difference, a decreasing of a value by another". If Numbers are provided, a subtraction is performed and the resulting Number is returned. If Vectors are provided, a subtraction is performed and the resulting Vector is returned. Whatever is returned is added to the top of the Akashic Stack._

**Example Spells:**

1 1 SINVARA DEBUG -> Allows the caster to see the Akashic and view the difference of 1 and 1 (which is 0).

ETE BIEGEA ETE ESILIB SINVARA DEBUG -> Allows the caster to see the Akashic and view the difference of their head position and their view direction.

This result represents the position directly behind the caster's head. By changing DEBUG to FIRUGEA DEBUG, the caster could see what block is directly behind them.

**TOCOVER**

**SYNTAX**

_Akashic -> Number/Vector, Number/Vector_

_Tocover always requires 2 Number Clusters or Vector Clusters. Numbers and Vectors can be mixed, and there is no predetermined order that must be followed._

_Akashic <- Number/Vector_

_Tocover is the incant that signifies "multiplication" and "scaling". If two Numbers are provided, their product is returned as the resulting Number. If two Vectors are provided, their product is returned as the resulting Vectors. If a Number and a Vector is provided, the Vector is scaled by that Number and the result will be the new scaled Vector. Whatever is returned is added to the top of the Akashic Stack._

**Example Spells:**

2 5 TOCOVER DEBUG -> Allows the caster to see the Akashic and view the product of 2 and 5 (which is 10).

ETE GEA ETE ESILIB TOCOVER DEBUG -> Allows the caster to see the Akashic and view the product of their foot position and their view direction. Why any magus might need this? No one knows.

ETE ESILIB 5 TOCOVER DEBUG -> Allows the caster to see the Akashic and view the product of their view direction and 5. This is simply the view vector with a length of 5. The following spell shows its use.

ETE ESILIB 5 TOCOVER ETE BIEGEA SINVERA FIRUGEA DEBUG -> Allows the caster to see the Akashic and see what block exists exactly 5 block in front of them. How this spell works will be left for you to decipher.

**TOCOVAR**

**SYNTAX**

_Akashic -> Number/Vector, Number/Vector_

_Tocovar always requires 2 Number Clusters or Vector Clusters. Numbers and Vectors can be mixed, and there is no predetermined order that must be followed. Never divide a number by 0._

_Akashic <- Number/Vector_

_Tocovar is the incant that signifies "division". If two Numbers are provided, their product is returned as the resulting Number. If two Vectors are provided, their quotient is returned as the resulting Vectors. If a Number and a Vector is provided, the Vector is divided by that Number and the result will be the new descaled Vector. Whatever is returned is added to the top of the Akashic Stack._

**Example Spells:**

10 2 TOCOVAR DEBUG -> Allows the caster to see the Akashic and view the quotient of 10 and 2 (which is 5).
