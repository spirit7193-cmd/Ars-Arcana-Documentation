There are times when questions are asked. At others, things must be confirmed. There are times when questions only have two possible answers: yes or no. In this section, the Incants representing both truth and lie will be documented.

**IRO**

**SYNTAX**

_Akashic -> No Clusters_

_Iro does not require clusters to function. It is constant in its own way._

_Akashic <- Boolean_

_Iro is the incant that signifies "false, lies, untruth". When it is encountered, a Boolean representing false is added to the top of the Akashic Stack._

**Example Spells:**

IRO DEBUG -> Allows the caster to see the Akashic and view a false Boolean cluster.

**IRA**

**SYNTAX**

_Akashic -> No Clusters_

_Ira does not require clusters to function. It is constant in its own way._

_Akashic <- Boolean_

_Ira is the incant that signifies "true, or truth". When it is encountered, a Boolean representing true is added to the top of the Akashic Stack._

**Example Spells:**

IRA DEBUG -> Allows the caster to see the Akashic and view a true Boolean cluster.
