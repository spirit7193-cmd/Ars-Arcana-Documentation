There are magicks that exist in this world that are older than words, older than civilization. This book documents none of them. Akashic Magick, named after the goddess Akasha, is one of the newer arts in the 3 Worlds, and it uses arcanic (a mystical language) to influence the world and create wonders. Naturally, this language comes with words, sounds (when verbalized) and letters combined that hold meaning to who and that which understands. 
That is this book's purpose; to serve as a dictionary for these incants (what these words are commonly called) so you may know what they mean. As you learn them, this book will naturally fill out itself. Additionally, each page will attempt to provide arcanic examples, to help provide much needed context to spellwriting.
