Some magical categorizations use the four classical elements. Others add and/or use ephemeral concepts like Mind and Spirit. A variety have no such chains and anything that is magical is simply that: magic. Akashic Magick, and Fae Magick in general, is one such magic. However, Magi adore the idea of boxing things in neat little boxes to such a degree that it is frightening, hence the recognition of the Cubes.

The Cubes of Magic are simply esoteric categorizations of Akashic Magick, based on Ancient Lores, Traits and Knowledges gleaned and attained via research. In my ancient opinion, they are never to be taken as law. After all, no Magi knows all the laws that Lady Akasha's occult creation abides by (though it does not prevent us from attempting to).

As you make Scribe Notes from books of questionable authors and theories, you might see mentions of some of them. Simply remember that they are ways to understand something that eludes full understanding.
