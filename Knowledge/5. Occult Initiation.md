Magic (to Magi) tends to be a principal Science, but also a mystical Art. They seem seperate but when both aspects are considered valid, they work in extraordinary tandem. A Magus' initiation ritual is that: a combination of high magick (as the witches call it) and and application of the philosophical divisions (to Magi) of the natural world.

 
 In practice, the rite is absurdly simple. It requires:

        -4 Torches, placed in a plus (+) formation with the center block empty.

        - An Ink & Quill, held in the hand and used while in the center of this Torch formation.

The initiating Magus should _not_ leave the central point of the torch "diamond". If they do, they risk the rite falling apart and failing.

After the rite is completed properly, the prospective Magus truly comes into his own. $3$ usually cannot immediately conjure and send off fire charges, but he does have the _potential_ to do so if he figures it out (or more rarely, is taught how).
