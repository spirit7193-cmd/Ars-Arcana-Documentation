_Within a Library of Everkilling Knowledge, there lives a god. He alone is the pinnacle of his ilk; and he alone has the Living Library's individual favor. After all, he is her dearest Librarian._

Sometimes, when a Magus with enough Bibliomantic traits performs normal research, the Librarian (otherwise known as Thoth) may look down upon them favorably. This grants them something called a Librarian Favor. A Magus may only have 8 such Favors at once.

With a Librarian Favor, a Magus may save a spell to the world itself as its own Thoth Incants. These are special Incants that function exactly like normal Incants, but will perform the saved spell when it is encountered in any spell across the dimensions. In addition, these words are usable by any Magus that casts it, so long as they know the Thoth Incant's name.

**Creating Thoth Incants**

With a Librarian Favor, all a Magus needs to do is write a Spell Page, obfuscate it using Ink & Sacs, and then sleep while holding it in their main hand. If it works, the Spell Page will vanish as if it was never there.

The Spell Page's name is what will serve as the Thoth Incant's name so ENSURE it is one word, otherwise nothing will happen.
