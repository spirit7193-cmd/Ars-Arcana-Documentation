Enchantment is the most commonly used magick in the world. It is used to augment armors, weapons and tools, allowing them to be better than they normally could be. However, Magi tend to use its Books to learn of Ancient Lores.

Ancient Lores are stories; stories written with occult intent. No one assumes everything written in them is true (that would be absurd), but clearly, they hold arcane significance. Researching them increases arcana capacity, sometimes even stimulating a change in occult knowledge and esoterica (giving rise to a Trait)

The stories tend to connect back to each other, much like a strange and unsettling spider web. The higher up on the spectrum a specific story is, the more important it _may_ be. Care should be taken when researching mind altering material though; gaining a Trait is never a fully positive event and they are very permanent.
