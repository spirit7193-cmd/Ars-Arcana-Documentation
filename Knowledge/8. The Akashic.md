Beyond the infinite darkness of Nowhere is Somewhere. There, the Things that Are sit within their domains, doing the things they do, and being the things they and their subjects wish them to be. Within this empty place of everything that shall never be wanders The Akashic.

Some call It a deity, others consider It a divine construct. The witches think It is Faerie (though, they are right. _Everything_ from Nowhere is Faerie but with a little uncanny charm). For the Magus, these classifications do not matter.

The Akashic is a Cosmic Chest.

It holds all Clusters that have ever been and will ever be. It does so for Its Lady, the Lady of the Library, Akasha. We, Magi, call upon Its attention to become Chests for Clusters ourselves. That is how we work Akashic Magick. We have found no other way.

The Akashic as the Cosmic Chest is the safest way it can be perceived by you. To do otherwise implies you are sane in a way most others could only consider insanity.
