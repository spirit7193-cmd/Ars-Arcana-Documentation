Sometimes, a researcher might end up taking notes while trying to gain insight into magic through literature. These notes are considered Scribe Notes.

These writings tend to be accepted as factual. After all, the Lady would not allow important knowledge to be obscured from those who seek and require it. In fact, they are considered important to read, as they provide information that I have hidden. I cannot obscure what is already known.

Scribe Notes are researchable, providing experience points, Sacrifices, and/or knowledge. Their ability to be turned into experience has allowed them to even be useful to the Mundane and the witches among them.
