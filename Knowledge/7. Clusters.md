Feats of magic primarily depend on information gathered to allow Incants to work properly. This information is offered in the form of Clusters.

Clusters represent the world in all its disjointed pieces. You are an Entity Cluster, the block under your feet is a Block Cluster, and the very position you are standing in can be represented as a Vector Cluster. Even I, as a book, may be considered an Item Cluster.

Clusters are everywhere because they are everything.

You will not find a list of all the different types of Clusters here. That is for you to discover and make a note of; however, common sense does apply.

A cat will provide an Entity Cluster. Sand will provide a Block Cluster. A sword akashically found in a chest is an Item Cluster, but one found on the floor is an Entity Cluster. A coordinate is a Vector Cluster.
