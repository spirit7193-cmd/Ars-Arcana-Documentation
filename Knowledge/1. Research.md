Research, for a promising Magus, is the analysis of books and other academic materials in order to construct a set of facts and/or come to new conclusions. Its purpose is simple: Akashic Magic begins and ends with what a Magus knows. The more they know, the more they understand, and the more they can do with magic.

Researching can be done at the Lectern with an Ink & Quill in the offhand and any researchable material in the mainhand. Books, Enchanted Books, Scribe Notes and Arcane Lores all count as researchable materials. By interacting with the Lectern, research is performed.

lnsight is primarily gained through this method. When a researcher gains 100+ insight, their arcana reserves increase by a rough 15%. However, exhaustion increases with each successful research attempt as well. Eventually, the researcher will become too tired to continue.
