There are various Incants that do truly magical things; like conjure things out of nothing and allow entities to step many blocks in an instant. In this section, you may glean the Clusters that power them and use them for your own purposes.

**GIMALIS (Projectile)**

**SYNTAX**

_Akashic -> Vector, Vector, List_

_Gimalis requires 3 Clusters to function properly._

_The Vector Cluster determines where the GIMALIS arrow is spawned._

_The Vector Cluster determines what direction the GIMALIS arrow will move in, as well as how fast (depends on the Vector's magnitude)._

_The List Cluster determines the Spell List that the GIMALIS arrow will cast on collision._

_Akashic <- No Clusters_

_Arcana Cost: 20 + Cost of the Spell List_

_Gimalis is the only known projectile Incant to exist, and it manifests as an arrow and shares all its attributes besides the fact that it shatters in collision. The Spell List is cast on the hit Entity or Block; that is, the arrow pulls the target's Cluster into the spell and attempts to affect it. Besides that, GIMALIS is a fairly decent damage spell._

**Example Spells:**

ETE BIEGEA ETE ESILIB ABI BIS VIR GIMALIS -> Fires an arrow that tries to cast BIS on the Block or Entity hit.

ETE BIEGEA ETE ESILIB ABI VIR GIMALIS -> Fires an arrow that casts nothing on the hit Entity or Block. This is simply a ranged damage spell.

**MALFAN (Blink)**

**SYNTAX**

_Akashic -> Entity, Vector, Number_

_Malfan requires 3 Clusters to function properly._

_The Entity Cluster determines what entity is affected by the Incant._

_The Vector Cluster determines what direction MALFAN will teleport the Entity in._

_The Number Cluster determines how far in the Vector’s direction the entity will teleport._

_Akashic <- No Clusters_

_Arcana Cost: 5 \* the distance teleported._

_Malfan is a teleportation Incant that roughly translates to "blink". With a Vector direction and a Number, the Entity is teleported that exact amount away in the specified direction. While some calculations may be involved, it is a fairly simple and cheap way to teleport; even if it can be dangerous._

**Example Spells:**

ETE ETE ESILIB 5 MALFAN -> Teleports the caster 5 blocks in their facing direction.

ETE ETE MALIBDIE IRAPAL 100 MALFAN -> Teleports the caster 100 blocks to their local east.

**MALFANEME (True Teleport)**

**SYNTAX**

_Akashic -> Entity, Vector_

_Malfaneme requires 2 Clusters to function properly._

_The Entity Cluster determines what entity is affected by the Incant._

_The Vector Cluster determines where the Entity will appear based on the coordinates._

_Akashic <- No Clusters_

_Arcana Cost: 250 \* the distance teleported._

_Malfaneme is a teleportation Incant that roughly translates to "true teleport". All that is required is a Vector coordinate, and with it, the Entity travels there instantaneously (provided their arcana holds true)._

**Example Spells:**

ETE 100 0 100 SINAFIL MALFANEME -> Teleports the caster to the coordinates (100, 0, 100).
