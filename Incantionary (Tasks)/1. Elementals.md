There are experts that believe that the world is made up of physical elements and forces. In a way, they are correct. Whatever the case may be, there are Incants that can conjure and influence these elements and forces in expected and unexpected ways.

**FUS (Daylight)**

**SYNTAX**

_Akashic -> Entity/Vector_

_Fus requires 1 Cluster to function properly._

_Akashic <- No Clusters_

_Arcana Cost: 10_

_FUS means "daylight" or "sunlight". Ironically, it simply conjures a small amorphous ball of light which does not seem like daylight at all when cast on a Vector._

_When cast on an Entity, specifically a Player, they are flashbanged. Regardless of the Entity however, this flash leaves them effectively blind for a short period._

**Example Spells:**

ETE GEA FUS -> Conjures a FUS light at the caster's position.

ETE FUS -> Flashbangs the caster.

**SINBERHUS (Lightning Arc)**

**SYNTAX**

_Akashic -> Entity, Vector_

_Sinberhus requires 2 Clusters to function properly._

_Akashic <- No Clusters_

_Arcana Cost: 30_

_SINBERHUS means "lightning arc". It doesn't literally mean that but that is what many magi consider it._

_With the Entity as the source point, an arc of electricity is sent off in the direction the Vector dictates. All entities in the way are damaged by the electricity, with the amount of damage dealt increasing the closer the entity is to the source (that is, the Entity used as the source point)._

**Example Spells:**

ETE SINJIT ESILIB SINBERHUS -> Fires off a lightning arc in the direction that the caster is looking in.

**SINBASHUS (Lightning Strike)**

**SYNTAX**

_Akashic -> Vector_

_Sinberhus requires 1 Cluster to function properly._

_Akashic <- No Clusters_

_Arcana Cost: 45_

_Sinbashus means "lightning strike". Like the other lightning incant, it doesn't literally mean that but that is what many magi consider it._

**Example Spells:**

ETE GEA SINBASHUS -> Strikes the caster with lightning.

**BISILIC (Explosion)**

**SYNTAX**

_Akashic -> Vector, Number, Boolean, Boolean_

_Bisilic requires 4 Clusters to function properly._ 

_A Vector to determine where the effect happens._

_A Number to determine how large or small the explosion is. It is recommended to keep this value low\._

_A Boolean to determine if the explosion is destructive or not._

_A Boolean to determine if the explosion leaves behind a fiery mess._

_Akashic <- No Clusters_

_Arcana Cost: Variable, but most times it is ALOT._

_Bisilic means "boom". Explosions. One of the most destructive Incants to exist. Archmagus Squiddable and Archmagus Xphere are the most notable users of its power._

_The above Cluster layout already explains how it works but essentially, BISILIC triggers an explosion._

**Example Spells:**

ETE GEA 3 IRA IRA BISILIC -> No explanation needed.

**XUIGIBMAL (Steal Velocity)**

**SYNTAX**

_Akashic -> Entity, Boolean_

_Xuigibmal requires 2 Clusters to function properly._ 

_Akashic <- Vector_

_Arcana Cost: 5 if set to true._

_Xuigibmal means "steal movement/velocity". The Entity’s velocity is snatched, forcing them to an immediate halt if the Boolean is set to "true". Regardless of whether the velocity is stolen physically, its Vector value is added to the top of the Akashic._

**Example Spells:**

ETE IRA XUIGIBMAL -> Steals the caster's velocity and adds it to the Akashic.

**MAL (Movement)**

**SYNTAX**

_Akashic -> Entity/Vector, Vector_

_Mal requires 2 Clusters to function properly._ 

_Akashic <- No Cluster_

_Arcana Cost: 10x the magnitude of the Vector._

_Mal represents "movement" or, as its more commonly known in context to spells, "kinetic shove". The Entity is shoved in the direction the Vector represents. The longer the Vector, the more powerful the push (though there are limits)._

_MAL performed over a Vector position with a block may give that block gravity. Sometimes, this proves useful for traps or unconventional ranged weapons._

**Example Spells:**

ETE ETE ESILIB MAL -> Nudges the caster forward.
