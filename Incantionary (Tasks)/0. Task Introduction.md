The Incants in the Passive section all manipulate the Akashic and only the Akashic. They create no physical change and summon no magical phenomenon. Task Incants are different.

To varying degrees, these Incants can change the world. Something new is added to it, or something old is taken/destroyed. Sometimes, both happen simultaneously.

Within this chapter lies the myriad of ways a magus affects the world. Few magi know them all and some are even heavily disliked, but none are ever quite useless.
